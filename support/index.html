Subscriptions

Event Budget Planner offers an optional auto-renewing subscription to unlock Pro features. To manage or cancel your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad, tap your name, then Subscriptions, select Event Budget Planner and follow the on-screen instructions. For additional guidance, see Apple’s subscription management guide.
